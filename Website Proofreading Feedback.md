## 🔵 Non-Copy Suggestions (Worth Flagging to Client)

**Suggestion 1 — Email address**
The publicly listed email (`aristotleascent.info@gmail.com`) is a free Gmail account. For a studio positioning itself as exclusive and premium, a custom domain email (`info@aristotleascent.com`) would meaningfully strengthen brand credibility. Worth raising with the client.

**Suggestion 2 — Operating hours**
The listed hours (Thu & Fri: 6–9 PM, Sat: 10 AM–4 PM) are quite limited. Consider adding a line such as *"Additional sessions available by appointment"* to avoid turning away prospective clients who may assume no flexibility exists.
