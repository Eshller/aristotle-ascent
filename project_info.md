**Prepared by**

**Client:** Aristotle Ascent – Education Counselling & Consulting  
**Date:** March 2026  

---

## Project Overview

Following the technical audit of the Aristotle Ascent website, several opportunities were identified to improve the platform’s functionality, performance, scalability, and ability to support student inquiries and marketing activities.

The current website provides a strong visual foundation and communicates the brand effectively. The objective of this redevelopment project is to transform the existing website into a fully functional digital platform capable of supporting lead generation, improved discoverability, and long-term growth.

This proposal outlines the scope of work and development timeline required to achieve these objectives.

---

## Project Objectives

The primary objectives of the website revamp are to:

- Improve the website’s ability to capture and manage student inquiries  
- Enhance search engine visibility  
- Strengthen the technical architecture for long-term scalability  
- Improve performance and reliability  
- Enhance user experience across devices  
- Prepare the platform for future marketing and content initiatives  

---

## Executive Summary

A technical audit of the Aristotle Ascent website was conducted to evaluate the platform’s design quality, usability, technical architecture, scalability, and readiness for long-term digital operations.

The current website successfully establishes a professional brand presence and communicates the organization’s services clearly. The visual design is refined and aligns well with the positioning of a premium education consulting service.

However, the website currently operates primarily as a static informational platform rather than a fully integrated digital system capable of supporting lead generation, marketing operations, and long-term scalability.

Key functional components such as automated inquiry handling, deeper search engine optimization, and scalable architecture have not yet been implemented.

Addressing these areas would significantly enhance the website’s ability to generate student inquiries, support marketing efforts, and scale with business growth.
